Cheese Pizza Recipe

Description

If you're craving a yummy pizza recipe, then this is the one for you! Made in our fiery ovens, baked at just the right temperature you will surely have a great time cooking! And, an amazing meal afterwards

To start you will need to prepare these ingredients.

Crucial Tools

  • Stand Mixer
  • Pizza Pan
  • Pizza Cutter

    • Pizza Dough Ingredients

    • 1 packet of yeast: Instant yeast can cook it faster
    • some water : warm water to cut down on rise time, about 105 degreesF-115 degreesF.
    • Flour: unbleached all-purpose white flour in the pizza crust
    • Oil: extra virgin olive oil add wonderful flavor to the dough
    • Salt: Add flavor
    • Sugar: a little sugar increases the yeasts activity and tenderizes the pizza dough
    • Cornmeal: Use cornmeal to dust the pizza pan; gives a little extra flavor and crisp

    Steps

    1. Prepare the pizza dough through step 6, including preheating the oven to 475 degreesF
    2. Top the dough with Pizza sauce, then the sliced mozzarella, grated parmesan, then sprinkle with basil
    3. Bake for 12-15min. For the last minute, you can move the oven rack the the top rack to brown the edges; optional.
    4. Slive hot pizza and serve immediatly. Cover leftover pizza tightly and store in the refrigerator. Reheat as you prefer.
