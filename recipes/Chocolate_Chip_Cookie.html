Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Description

So you're craving some cookies. What better way to enjoy them than to make them yourself right in your house! These chocolate chip cookies are the best in the world!

Prepare the ingredients below to begin!

Ingredients for 28 count of cookies

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • half teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water.
  5. Add to batter along with salt.
  6. Stir in flour, chocolate chops, and nuts.
  7. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  8. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
